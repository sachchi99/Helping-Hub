Bike Brake Problem

If your bike is experiencing brake problems, it's crucial to address them promptly. Brake issues can compromise your safety on the road. Common brake problems include:

  • Squeaking or squealing brakes
  • Soft or mushy brake pedal
  • Brake fluid leakage
  • Reduced stopping power

If you are experiencing any of these brake problems, it's essential to have a professional mechanic inspect and repair your bike's braking system.
